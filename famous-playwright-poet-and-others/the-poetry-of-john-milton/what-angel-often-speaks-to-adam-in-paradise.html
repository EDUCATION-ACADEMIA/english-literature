What angel often speaks to Adam in Paradise?

(A) Michelangelo

(B) ** Raphael

(C) Pandosto

(D) Baal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Meanwhile, in Heaven, God calls the Archangel Raphael to his side. He does not want Adam and Eve to claim that the devil took them by surprise if they are lured into disobedience, so he instructs Raphael to tell Adam about the danger in store for him.

Concept note-2: -Finally, Raphael admonishes Adam to remember God’s warning and to be on guard for Satan’s treachery. He also tells Adam not to allow passion to overcome reason and cause him to disobey God. With that, the two beings, man and angel, part; Raphael toward Heaven, Adam to his bower.

Concept note-3: -In the Garden of Eden, Adam and Eve meet the Angel Raphael and the angel warns them not to disobey God’s commands and that Satan is plotting to bring about the fall.

Concept note-4: -Michael the Archangel. In Paradise Lost Milton sets up the Archangel Michael as the polar opposite of Satan, leading the forces loyal to God.
