What is another word for ‘crowd’ in the poem?

(A) group

(B) cluster

(C) ** host

(D) golden

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Some common synonyms of crowd are crush, horde, mob, and throng.

Concept note-2: -synonyms for the in-crowd aristocracy. beau monde. café society. cream of society. crème de la crème.

Concept note-3: -A crowd is a large group of people or an audience. Crowd also means to cram closely together. Crowd has several other senses as a noun and a verb. The word crowd can be used to describe any large group of people. Often, crowd is used to specifically mean that the people are standing close together.

Concept note-4: -1. countable noun [with singular or plural verb] A crowd is a large group of people who have gathered together, for example to watch or listen to something interesting, or to protest about something.

Concept note-5: -: ordinary people : people who are not viewed or treated as special or unusual. kids trying to distinguish themselves from the crowd. She prefers to be one of the crowd.
