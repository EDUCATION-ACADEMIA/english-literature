Concept note-1: -Alfred Prufrock” in 1915, which, at the time of its publication, was considered outlandish. He was also known for seven plays, particularly Murder in the Cathedral (1935) and The Cocktail Party (1949).

Concept note-2: -Eliot best known for? T.S. Eliot was an American-English poet, playwright, literary critic, and editor. He is best known as a leader of the Modernist movement in poetry and as the author of such works as The Waste Land (1922) and Four Quartets (1943).
