easily upset by things people think or say

(A) mature

(B) ** sensitive

(C) dependable

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -easily upset by the things people say or do, or causing people to be upset, embarrassed, or angry: Her reply showed that she was very sensitive to criticism.

Concept note-2: -Irritable, testy, touchy, irascible are adjectives meaning easily upset, offended, or angered. Irritable means easily annoyed or bothered, and it implies cross and snappish behavior: an irritable clerk, rude and hostile; Impatient and irritable, he was constantly complaining.

Concept note-3: -↪ One who easily gets upset by the comments and reactions of people is known as thin-skinned .

Concept note-4: -hypersensitive. adjective. very easily upset or offended.
