Concept note-1: -Informational text is used by authors to provide readers with information about a specific topic. This type of writing is usually nonfiction and has specific features. The author will use headers over certain sections, bold key vocabulary or provide visual representations with captions.

Concept note-2: -The 5 Kinds of Nonfiction include active, browsable, traditional, expository literature, and narrative nonfiction.

Concept note-3: -Informational text is a subset of the larger category of nonfiction (Duke & Bennett-Armistead, 2003). Its primary purpose is to inform the reader about the natural or social world. Different from fiction, and other forms of nonfiction, informational text does not utilize characters.
