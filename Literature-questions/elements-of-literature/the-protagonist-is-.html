The protagonist is ____

(A) The good guy

(B) The bad guy

(C) ** The main character, the one with the problem, the one you are rooting for

(D) The person who seems to be the most positive about everything, the person with the best attitude

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We all have some type of protagonist and antagonist in our life. The protagonist is the person you are rooting for, while the antagonist is that one person who makes it hard to root for them.

Concept note-2: -The protagonist is the character who drives the actionâ€“the character whose fate matters most. In other words, they are involved in-and often central to-the plot or conflict of the story, but are also usually the emotional heart of the narrative.

Concept note-3: -A Main Character is the player through whom the audience experiences the story first hand. A Protagonist is the prime mover of the plot. A Hero is a combination of both Main Character and Protagonist.

Concept note-4: -Conflict is a problem that must be solved; an issue between the protagonist and antagonist forces. It forms the basis of the plot.

Concept note-5: -The protagonist of a story is the leading or main character in a book, movie, short story, play, or other works of fiction. They are the person the story centers around and the character readers will root for to succeed.
