What did Mr Herriot instruct to Mrs Pumphery?

(A) ** for reducing sweet things

(B) for reducing exercise

(C) for reducing cod liver oil

(D) for reducing Horlicks

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Herriot’s strict advice was to cut down on sweet things and to give him plenty of exercises. But Mrs. Pumphrey did not heed (pay attention) to this advice. She thought Tricki was weak.

Concept note-2: -Herriot warned Mrs. Pumphrey that if she didn’t cut his food right down and give him more exercise he is going to be really ill. He advised her to harden her heart and keep Tricki on a very strict diet.

Concept note-3: -Answer: Dr Herriot was worried about Tricki because he had become hugely fat and inactive. He suggested Mrs Pumphrey to cut down his food and give him some exercise, but she was reluctant to do so.

Concept note-4: -Mr. Herriot’s strict advice was to cut down Tricki’s diet and give him a lot of water and more exercise to avoid illness. But Mrs. Pumphrey did not pay heed to his advice. She made an excuse that Tricki seemed so weak that she had to relent and could not refuse him.

Concept note-5: -Pumphrey called Mr. Herriot as expected. She called him because Tricki had stopped eating. He refused to eat his favorite dishes.
